## Gameplay: Four Stages of Transcendence

### **STAGE 1: CELLULAR SURVIVAL** (Spore meets Dark Souls)
You start as a single microscopic cell in primordial soup. Around you ‚Äî enemies, resources, danger. You gather fats, proteins, carbs. Fight other cells. Evolve.

Three paths:
- **Aggression** (red) ‚Äî spikes, poison, predator machine
- **Adaptation** (green) ‚Äî armor, photosynthesis, survival in any condition
- **Stealth** (blue) ‚Äî camouflage, speed, ambush from darkness

Win: reach critical mass or eliminate rivals.

### **STAGE 2: TRIBE** (Tribal Conquest)
Now you're a **colony**. You build structures from food particles. Create castes: workers, warriors, scientists. Fight for refrigerator territory.

### **STAGE 3: CIVILIZATION** (Tech Trees & Diplomacy)
You have cities, technologies, resource networks. Either conquer neighbors or trade. Research. Build.

This is **macrogameplay** now.

### **STAGE 4: TRANSCENDENCE** (The Ultimate Form)
You escape the fridge. Conquer the kitchen. Then your home. Your civilization becomes **perfection**.
